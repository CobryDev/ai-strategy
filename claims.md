## Notes

1. **Sources marked ⚠️** could not be independently verified via web search. This doesn't mean they're wrong — many are behind paywalls (Gartner, Forrester, McKinsey), are internal company reports, or are from smaller/niche publishers that didn't surface in search results.

2. **Some claims aggregate multiple sources.** For example, "77% of workers say AI increased workload" is attributed to a survey of 2,500 professionals but the specific survey name wasn't given in the guide.

3. **Approximate count:** The guide contains **200-300 distinct factual claims** from **60-80 distinct sources**. Of these, roughly **15-20 major sources** are fully verified with links above, and **40-60 smaller sources** would require additional research.

4. **Recommendation:** For claims you plan to use in final publication, prioritize verifying the ⚠️ sources by contacting the original publishers, searching academic databases (Google Scholar, SSRN), or checking paywalled analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC) through institutional access.
